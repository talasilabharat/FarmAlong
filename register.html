REGISTRATION PROCESS

Register for Agri E Smart Card:


You can now register for Agri E-Smart Card by visiting your nearest Gram-Panchayat. Remember you need to carry your Aadhar Card and Pass Book for registration. After successful registration you will get an SMS to your registered phone number. After Cross Verification with-in 60 days you will receive your Agri E-Smart Card to your registered home address, in Courier along with the instructions of how to Use it.

Thank You. Jai Kisan
